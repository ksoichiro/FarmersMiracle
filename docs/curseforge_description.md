## üåæ Farmer's Miracle - Collect Sacred Orbs, Bless Your Crops

**Explore temples, gather three sacred orbs, and permanently boost your crop growth!**

Farmer's Miracle adds three unique temple structures to your world, each containing a collectible orb. Gather all three orbs to unlock permanent crop growth buffs that affect nearby crops as you farm.

---

## ‚ú® Features

### üèõÔ∏è 3 Temple Structures
- **Temple of Golden Wheat** ‚Äî A shrine dedicated to wheat, hidden in the wilderness
- **Temple of Pumpkins** ‚Äî A temple celebrating the pumpkin harvest
- **Temple of Melons** ‚Äî A tropical temple honoring melons
- All temples generate within 2000 blocks of the initial spawn point
- Minimum 300+ blocks distance between temples
- Found in plains, forests, taigas, savannas, meadows, and more

### üîÆ Collectible Orbs
Each temple chest contains a unique orb:
- **Wheat Orb** ‚Äî from the Temple of Golden Wheat
- **Pumpkin Orb** ‚Äî from the Temple of Pumpkins
- **Melon Orb** ‚Äî from the Temple of Melons

### üèÜ Advancements
- Triggered by obtaining each orb
- A final advancement for collecting all three orbs

### üå± Permanent Crop Growth Buffs
Each advancement grants **"Blessing of Grains"** buffs:
- **Growth I‚ÄìIV**: Increases crop growth speed (stacks with each orb collected, +3% per level)
- **Spread I**: Expands buff range from 16 to 24 blocks (granted by collecting all 3 orbs)
- **Target crops**: Wheat, Pumpkin, Melon, Potato, Carrot, Beetroot
- Buffs are permanent and persist across sessions

### ü§ù Mod Compatibility
Crop growth buffs also apply to grain and vegetable crops from the following mods (no additional setup required):
- **Farmer's Delight** (`farmersdelight`) ‚Äî Cabbages, Onions, Tomatoes, Rice
- **Croptopia** (`croptopia`) ‚Äî Barley, Corn, Oat, Rice, and many more vegetables
- **Pam's HarvestCraft 2 - Crops** (`pamhc2crops`) ‚Äî Barley, Corn, Rice, Rye, and many more vegetables
- **Cultural Delights** (`culturaldelights`) ‚Äî Corn, Cucumbers, Eggplants

> **Note**: Only grain and vegetable crops are included by default. Fruits, herbs, and beverages are not covered. You can add support for additional crops or mods by creating a datapack that appends entries to the `farmersmiracle:grains` block tag.

## üìñ How to Play

1. **Explore the World**: Search for temples near the world spawn
2. **Find the Temples**: Look for the three unique temple structures in various biomes
3. **Collect the Orbs**: Open the chest in each temple to obtain its orb
4. **Earn Advancements**: Each orb triggers an advancement and grants a crop growth buff
5. **Collect All Three**: Gathering all three orbs unlocks the maximum buff level and expanded range
6. **Farm with Power**: Enjoy permanently boosted crop growth around you!
